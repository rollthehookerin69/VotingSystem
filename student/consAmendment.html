Constitutional Amendment to allow pets in University Residence Halls

This Constitutional Amendment pertains to Article 7 section 3 which it would overide.

The section presently reads:
Due to concerns with allergies and living in close quarters with animals no pets will be allowed in Morgantown University Residence Halls

It would be replaced by:

Pets will be allowed in the Residence Halls under the following conditions:

  • All other tenants of the room agree to the presence of the pet in writing
  • A $20 fee is paid monthly by the pet owner to offset costs to the University resulting from the presence of a pet
